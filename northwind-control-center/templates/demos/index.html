Chapter 14 — Distributed Consensus: PoW vs Raft (rqlite · Chaos Monkey)

Three roles: Leader (accepts all writes), Follower (applies log entries), Candidate (runs election on heartbeat timeout). Randomized election timeouts (150–300 ms) prevent split-vote deadlocks. Majority acknowledgment = committed write.
