A CEX is NOT a blockchain. It is a microservices architecture: an in-memory Order Book (Redis) matches buy/sell orders via price-time priority, each trade event is published to Kafka for downstream consumers, and final balances are settled atomically in a PostgreSQL ACID ledger. Three FinTech rules apply: partial fills (orders stay open until fully consumed), locked-fund validation (available = ledger − open orders), and maker price (the older order sets the trade price).
