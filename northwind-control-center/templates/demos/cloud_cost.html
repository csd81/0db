▶ Cloud Cost Calculator IaaS vs PaaS vs CaaS vs FaaS — total monthly cost comparison

The economics of IaaS, PaaS, CaaS, and FaaS depend entirely on traffic shape: IaaS VMs cost the same whether serving 1 request or 10,000 per second; FaaS Cloud Functions bill per invocation and scale to zero when idle. Interactive sliders for requests per day, average latency, memory allocation, and storage translate architectural choices into monthly dollar amounts, making cost crossover points immediately visible. A low-traffic API with spiky weekend peaks is dramatically cheaper on FaaS; a sustained high-throughput pipeline running 24/7 costs less on reserved IaaS or managed containers. This model shows why 'serverless is always cheaper' is a myth and why the right deployment model depends entirely on request volume and duration.
