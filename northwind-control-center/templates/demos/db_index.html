DBMS Encyclopedia

Deep-dives for every major database system — 35+ systems across 12 categories

Modern applications rarely use a single database. Each system was designed to excel at a specific trade-off: relational databases guarantee ACID correctness, key-value stores deliver sub-millisecond reads, columnar engines scan billions of rows for analytics, and vector databases power AI semantic search.
