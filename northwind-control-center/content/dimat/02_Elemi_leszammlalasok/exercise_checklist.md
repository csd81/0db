## 📝 Key Formulas to Memorize

```
□ Pₙ = n!
□ Pₙ^(k₁,...,kₛ) = n!/(k₁!...kₛ!)
□ Vₙᵏ = n!/(n-k)!
□ Vₙᵏ⁽ⁱˢᵐ⁾ = nᵏ
□ Cₙᵏ = (n choose k) = n!/(k!(n-k)!)
□ Cₙᵏ⁽ⁱˢᵐ⁾ = (n+k-1 choose k)
□ Stirling: n! ≈ √(2πn)·(n/e)ⁿ
```
