## 📝 Key Formulas to Memorize

```
□ De Morgan: (A ∪ B)' = A' ∩ B', (A ∩ B)' = A' ∪ B'
□ Duality: Swap ∪↔∩, ∅↔I
□ Independence: |I| ≥ 2ⁿ for n independent sets
□ Boolean size: |B| ≤ 2^(2^m) for m generators
□ DNF: OR of ANDs
□ CNF: AND of ORs
```
