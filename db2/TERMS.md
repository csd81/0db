## General Advice for Translating IT Course Materials

1. Keep English industry-standard terms where appropriate. In Hungarian IT education, it is often standard practice to leave some words in English, for example: `Job`, `Trigger`, `Dump`, `Commit`, `Rollback`, `Collation`.
2. Always check context. A word may have a physical meaning and a software meaning. For example: `Table`, `Leaf`, `Dump`, `Log`, `Coupling`. Machine translation often chooses the physical meaning by default.
